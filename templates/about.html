Our Mission

Our Story

How DeepFake Shield was born and evolved

Jan 2023

Concept Development

Recognizing the growing threat of synthetic media, three computer science students began researching deepfake detection techniques.

Jun 2023

First Prototype

Developed initial AI models for detecting facial manipulation in videos and images with 85% accuracy.

Oct 2023

Platform Launch

Released the first public version of DeepFake Shield with video and image analysis capabilities.

Present

Continuous Improvement

Expanding detection capabilities to include audio deepfakes and improving accuracy with advanced neural networks.

Our Technology

Powered by cutting-edge AI and machine learning

Neural Networks

Advanced convolutional neural networks trained on millions of real and synthetic media samples.

Signal Analysis

Digital signal processing techniques to detect inconsistencies in audio and video streams.

Ensemble Learning
